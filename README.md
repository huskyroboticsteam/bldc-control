note for future Becca:
The 6x mode pwm sends one pwm to one gate and one high signal to the other gate (alternating with phases), not through the whole half bridge. The half bridge has a constant voltage and the pwm controls how quickly/slowly the "switch" (mosfet) opens and closes (essentially creating a new mathcing pwm signal through the half bridge from the constant voltage.

... I repeat, the pwm generated by the board does not go through the half bridge from the board, it creates a new pwm by switching open and closed the mosfet at differing rates

(additional note we are using 6x mode since we think its cool to control the mosfets but the driver controls other modes (like 1x) that can just take in a hall sensor and single pwm signal and is smart and figures it all out for!)
